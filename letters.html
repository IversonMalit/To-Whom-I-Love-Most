Letter

Dear Labs,

I hope you know how important you are to me,

you will always be my one and only. No matter what,

I will always be right here beside you. Through every hardship,

achievement, happiness, and moments of doubt. I'll stand with you.

 

You've become a big part of my life, and I am grateful for every moment I spend with you.

Every smile, every laugh, and even when things become heavy.

All the memories, I will cherish them as long as it's with you.

Your presence brings me peace, comfort, strength, and the will to carry on every day.

 

Always remember that my support for you isn't temporary,

it's unwavering and unbreaking. I'll always believe in you.

I will be the one to remind you of your worth, whatever the future holds.

We will face it together. Hand in hand, with patience, trust, love, and faith being our guide.
