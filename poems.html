"My Bounded Heart" For her alone, my every part, Every thought of her is an art; In every dawn, through the dark, only she can leave a mark. Her beauty blinds me, My love for her grows every day; an unending tree. One reaching the heaven, I thank the blessing; for her, to me, she was given. I am rich; Not with wealth nor fortunes told; but her love, is dearer far than all gold.
